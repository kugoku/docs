Before you build your first site, it helps to understand how a Goleme account is structured. This page covers sign-up, the credit wallet that powers Golem's AI, and the concept of projects — the core unit of work in Goleme.

## Creating your account

Go to [goleme.ai](https://goleme.ai) and click **Try it free**. You have two ways to sign up:

- **Email and password** — enter your email address, choose a password, and verify your email.
- **Google** — click **Continue with Google** and authorize access with your Google account.

Both methods create the same type of account. Once you're in, you'll land on your dashboard.

<Tip>
  Every new account receives a free welcome credit bonus. This is added to your wallet automatically at sign-up and gives you enough AI credit to build and preview a site without entering any payment details.
</Tip>

## The credit wallet

Golem uses AI to build and update your website. This AI usage is powered by credits, which are stored in your credit wallet.

- **Credits are consumed** each time Golem builds or makes significant changes to a site.
- **Your wallet balance** is visible in the top navigation bar of the dashboard.
- **Top up anytime** by going to **Billing → Credits** and adding funds — all plans support unlimited top-ups.

Credits are separate from your subscription plan. Your plan controls how many projects and custom domains you can have; credits control how much AI work you can request. When your wallet runs low, top it up to continue building.

## Understanding projects

A **project** is a single website in Goleme. Each project has its own AI chat history, pages, settings, and published URL.

Your plan determines how many projects you can have active at once:

| Plan | Projects | Custom domains |
|---|---|---|
| Explorer (free) | 3 | None |
| Business (1,199 CZK/yr) | 5 | 1 |
| Agency (4,199 CZK/yr) | 10 | 5 |
| Tailored | Unlimited | Unlimited |

You can create a new project from the dashboard at any time, up to your plan's limit. Each project is independent — changes in one project don't affect any other.

## Dashboard overview

After signing in, your dashboard is the central hub for everything in Goleme.

**Projects** — the main area shows all your active projects as cards. Each card displays the site name, its published URL (or draft status), and a button to open the builder. Click **New project** to start a fresh site.

**AI chat builder** — clicking into a project opens the chat interface where you talk to Golem. Your full conversation history is saved per project, so you can return and continue refining your site at any time.

**Billing** — the **Billing** section in the left sidebar gives you access to your subscription plan, your credit wallet balance, and top-up options.

**Settings** — each project has its own settings panel where you can rename it, connect a custom domain, or delete it.
