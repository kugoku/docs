Goleme lets anyone create a professional website without writing a single line of code. You describe your idea in plain language, and Golem — the AI assistant at the heart of Goleme — builds the site for you. Hosting is included from day one, and your site goes live on a `yoursite.goleme.ai` subdomain the moment you publish.

## How it works

Building a website with Goleme takes three steps. From your first message to a live URL, the whole process typically takes under ten minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your vision">
    Tell Golem what kind of website you need. Be as specific or as broad as you like — describe your business, the pages you want, the style you have in mind, or just paste a rough idea. Golem understands natural language, so there's no special syntax to learn. This step takes about one minute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Golem builds it">
    Golem goes to work. It generates your pages, layout, content, and styling based on your description. Depending on the complexity of your site, this takes between one and five minutes. You'll see progress in real time as your site takes shape.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch it">
    Review your site and publish it with one click. Your site goes live on a `yoursite.goleme.ai` subdomain instantly. If you want a custom domain, you can connect one through your project settings. Launching takes about one minute.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you can build

Goleme works for a wide range of website types. You can start from scratch by describing exactly what you need, or pick one of the ready-made templates to get a head start.

| Template | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Hair Salon** | Services, pricing, and booking for salons and beauty businesses |
| **Travel Blog** | Content-focused blogs with articles and posts |
| **E-Shop** | Full-featured online stores with products, orders, and an admin panel |
| **Craftsman** | Presentation sites for tradesmen, freelancers, and service businesses |

You're not limited to these categories. Golem can build portfolio sites, landing pages, restaurant menus, event pages, and more — just describe what you need.

## No technical skills required

You don't need to know HTML, CSS, or any programming language to use Goleme. You don't need to configure a server, set up hosting, or manage deployments. Everything from building to publishing happens through a conversation with Golem. After your site is live, you can keep editing it by opening the chat and describing what you want to change.
