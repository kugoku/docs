Publishing is the step that takes your site from a private draft to a live website that anyone can visit. Until you publish, your site exists only inside your Goleme project — no one else can see it. When you hit publish, Goleme deploys the current state of your site to its URL and makes it accessible on the web.

<Tip>
You can preview your site before publishing to check how it looks to visitors. Use the **Preview** button in your project to open a private preview link. The preview is not indexed by search engines and is not accessible to the public.
</Tip>

## What publishing does

When you publish a project, Goleme:

- Deploys the current version of your site to its URL
- Issues an SSL certificate (if one is not already active), so your site loads over `https://`
- Makes the site publicly accessible to anyone with the link

Publishing does not lock your site. You can continue editing and re-publish at any time.

## Publish to your Goleme subdomain

Every project has a free subdomain at `yoursite.goleme.ai` that is ready to use immediately — no DNS setup required.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Go to your [Goleme dashboard](https://goleme.ai) and select the project you want to publish.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview your site (optional)">
    Click **Preview** to open a private preview of your site. Check your pages, links, and layout before making the site public.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Publish">
    Click the **Publish** button in the project toolbar. Goleme deploys your site and confirms when it is live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visit your site">
    Your site is now live at `yoursite.goleme.ai`. Click the link shown in the dashboard to open it in your browser.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Publish to a custom domain

If you have connected a custom domain to your project, you can publish directly to that domain. The steps are the same as above — Goleme automatically serves your site at the connected custom domain once you publish.

<Note>
You must connect a custom domain before you can publish to it. If you have not done that yet, see [Connect a custom domain](/domains/custom-domain) first. Custom domains are available on Business and Agency plans.
</Note>

## Re-publishing after edits

Goleme does not publish changes automatically. Every time you update your site — whether you changed a heading, added a page, or adjusted the layout by chatting with Golem — you need to publish again to push those changes live.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Make your changes">
    Chat with Golem to update your site. You can change text, colors, structure, or add new sections and pages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview the changes (optional)">
    Click **Preview** to review the updated site before it goes live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish again">
    Click **Publish**. Goleme deploys the new version of your site. Your previous version is immediately replaced by the updated one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Visitors to your site will see the latest published version as soon as deployment completes, which typically takes a few seconds.
