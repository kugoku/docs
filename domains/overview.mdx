Every site you build with Goleme is hosted automatically — there is nothing to set up and no server to manage. As soon as you publish a project, it goes live at a URL that Goleme provides. Depending on your plan, you can keep that free subdomain or point your own domain name to the site instead.

## Your free subdomain

Every account, including the free Explorer plan, gets a subdomain under `goleme.ai`. When you create a project, Goleme assigns it a URL in this format:

```
yoursite.goleme.ai
```

The subdomain is based on your project name. You can publish to this address immediately without any DNS configuration or additional setup.

## Custom domains

If you want visitors to reach your site at your own domain — for example, `yourshop.cz` or `www.mybusiness.com` — you can connect a custom domain to any project. Custom domains are available on paid plans only.

<Warning>
Custom domain support is not available on the free Explorer plan. You need a Business or Agency plan to connect your own domain.
</Warning>

## Plan comparison

The table below shows what each plan includes for hosting and domains.

| Plan | Hosting | Goleme subdomain | Custom domains |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Explorer** (free) | Included | `yoursite.goleme.ai` | — |
| **Business** (1,199 Kč/year) | Included | `yoursite.goleme.ai` | 1 |
| **Agency** (4,199 Kč/year) | Included | `yoursite.goleme.ai` | 5 |
| **Tailored** | Included | `yoursite.goleme.ai` | Unlimited |

Business and Agency plans also include a yearly bonus credit to register or renew one `.cz` domain for one year through the Goleme platform.

## Domain ownership

When you register a domain through Goleme, it is registered by Kugoku s.r.o. (the company behind Goleme) on your behalf. You receive the right to use the domain for your site, but legal ownership of the domain stays with Kugoku s.r.o. If you bring an existing domain you already own from another registrar, you retain full ownership — you are only connecting it to Goleme for routing.
