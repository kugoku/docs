Below you will find answers to the most common questions about billing, credits, domains, and account management. If you have a question that is not covered here, [contact the Goleme team](https://goleme.ai/contact).

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Can I try Goleme before paying?">
    Yes. The Explorer plan is free and has no time limit. You also receive a welcome credit bonus when you sign up, which gives you enough credits to build your first site and see Golem in action before spending anything.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need a credit card to sign up?">
    No. You can create an account and start building on the free Explorer plan without providing any payment details. A payment method is only required when you purchase AI credits or upgrade to a paid plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
    Payments are processed through Goleme's payment system. Accepted methods are shown at checkout when you top up credits or select a paid plan. All transactions are in Czech Crowns (Kč).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I bring my own domain?">
    Yes. The Business plan includes one custom domain slot and the Agency plan includes five. You can point any domain you already own to your Goleme site. On the free Explorer plan, your site is hosted on a `yoursite.goleme.ai` subdomain.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I run out of credits?">
    If your credit balance reaches zero, Golem pauses before starting new AI build or edit actions. Your published websites stay live and fully accessible to visitors — running out of credits does not take your site offline. Top up your wallet at any time to resume building and editing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?">
    Yes. You can change your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the end of your current billing period so you keep the benefits you have already paid for.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are prices VAT-inclusive?">
    Yes. All Goleme plan prices are listed in Czech Crowns (Kč) and include VAT. There are no hidden charges added at checkout.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I own the domain registered through Goleme?">
    Domains registered through the Goleme platform are registered by Kugoku s.r.o. (the company behind Goleme) on your behalf. You receive the right to use the domain for your site for the duration of your subscription. If you register a domain through an external registrar and connect it to Goleme, you retain full ownership of that domain.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the yearly domain bonus?">
    Business and Agency subscribers receive a bonus after each successful yearly payment. You can use this bonus to register a new `.cz` domain or renew an existing one for one year at no extra cost. The bonus applies to one `.cz` domain per billing year.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    You can cancel your paid plan at any time from **Account settings → Subscription**. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period. After that, your account moves to the free Explorer plan — your projects are preserved, but domain connections and any features above the Explorer limits are paused.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    If you have a question about a charge or would like to request a refund, [contact the Goleme support team](https://goleme.ai/contact) with your account details. Goleme reviews refund requests on a case-by-case basis.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
