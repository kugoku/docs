AI credits are the currency that powers every action Golem takes on your behalf. When you describe a new site, ask Golem to redesign a page, or request a new feature, Golem consumes credits from your wallet to complete the work. Credits are separate from your plan price — your plan covers hosting and project limits, while credits cover the AI usage itself.

<Note>
  New Goleme accounts receive a welcome credit bonus so you can try the builder right away. No credit card is required to claim it.
</Note>

## When credits are consumed

Credits are deducted each time you trigger an AI action. Common actions that consume credits include:

- **Building a new site** — generating a complete website from your description
- **Editing your site** — asking Golem to change text, colors, layout, or add a new page or feature
- **Major redesigns** — requesting a significant structural overhaul of an existing site

Browsing your published site, viewing your dashboard, and managing settings do not consume credits.

## Checking your balance

Your current credit balance is always visible in the top navigation bar of your Goleme dashboard. Click the balance indicator to open your wallet and see a full history of credit purchases and usage.

## Topping up your wallet

When your balance runs low, top up directly from your wallet:

1. Open your dashboard and click your credit balance.
2. Select **Top up**.
3. Choose the amount you want to add.
4. Complete payment — the credits are added to your wallet immediately.

All credit amounts and wallet balances are displayed in Czech Crowns (Kč).

<Tip>
  Every plan — including the free Explorer plan — supports unlimited credit top-ups. You never need to upgrade your plan just to buy more credits.
</Tip>

## What happens when you run out

If your credit balance reaches zero, Golem will pause before starting new AI actions. Your published sites remain live and fully accessible to your visitors — running out of credits does not take your site offline. Top up your wallet to resume building and editing.

## The welcome bonus

When you create a new Goleme account, you receive a free credit bonus automatically. No credit card is required. The bonus is enough to let you describe and build a first website so you can see Golem in action before deciding whether to purchase more credits or upgrade your plan.

## Credits and plans

Credits are not bundled with any plan — they are always purchased separately. This means you only pay for the AI usage you actually need, regardless of which plan you are on. See the [Plans](/billing/plans) page for a full comparison of what each plan includes.
