Goleme offers four plans so you can start for free and scale as your needs grow. Every plan gives you access to the full AI builder — the main difference is how many projects you can run and whether you can connect a custom domain. AI credit consumption is billed separately on top of any plan price.

<Note>
  All prices are in Czech Crowns (Kč) and include VAT.
</Note>

## Plan comparison

## Plan details

### Explorer — free

Explorer is the best way to try Goleme without spending anything. You get three projects hosted on a free `yoursite.goleme.ai` subdomain. There is no time limit — stay on Explorer for as long as you like. You can top up AI credits at any time to keep building and editing.

### Business — 1,199 Kč/year

Business is the most popular plan for shops, services, and personal brands that want a professional web address. You get five projects and one custom domain slot so you can deploy a site at your own domain. After each successful yearly payment, Goleme gives you a bonus credit to register or renew one `.cz` domain for a year.

### Agency — 4,199 Kč/year

Agency is built for developers and agencies managing websites for multiple clients. You get ten projects and five custom domain slots, plus the same yearly `.cz` domain bonus as Business. Build and hand off sites without hitting project or domain limits.

### Tailored — contact sales

If you need more projects, more domains, or custom contractual terms, Goleme can build a plan around your requirements. [Contact the sales team](https://goleme.ai/contact) to discuss your needs.

## AI credits are separate from your plan

Your plan covers hosting and the number of projects and domains you can run. It does **not** include AI credit consumption. Credits are the currency Golem uses to build and edit your sites — you purchase them separately and top up your wallet whenever you need more. All plans support unlimited top-ups.

See [AI Credits](/billing/credits) for a full explanation of how credits work.

## Upgrade or downgrade

You can change your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately. When you downgrade, the change applies at the end of your current billing period.
