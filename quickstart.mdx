This guide walks you through everything from creating your Goleme account to publishing a live website. You don't need a credit card to get started, and no technical experience is required.

<Note>
  Goleme's free Explorer plan includes AI credit so you can try the full build experience without entering payment details.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [goleme.ai](https://goleme.ai) and sign up. You can register with an email address and password, or sign in with your Google account. After signing up, Goleme adds free credit to your wallet automatically — you're ready to build straight away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new project">
    From your dashboard, click **New project**. You'll be asked whether you want to start from scratch or use a template.

    - **From scratch** — start with a blank canvas and describe your site to Golem.
    - **From a template** — choose one of the ready-made starting points: Hair Salon, Travel Blog, E-Shop, or Craftsman. Templates give Golem a structural head start and speed up the build.

    Give your project a name, then continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your site to Golem">
    You'll land in the AI chat builder. Type a description of the website you want. Include details like:

    - The purpose of the site (e.g. "a website for my plumbing business")
    - The pages you need (e.g. "a home page, a services page, and a contact form")
    - Any style preferences (e.g. "clean and professional, dark color scheme")
    - Content you want included (e.g. your business name, location, or services)

    The more detail you provide, the closer Golem's first draft will be to what you have in mind. You can always refine it later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Golem to build">
    After you send your description, Golem starts building. You'll see a progress indicator as it generates your pages, layout, and content. Most sites are ready in one to five minutes depending on complexity.

    When Golem finishes, a preview of your site appears. Review it and, if you want changes, describe them in the chat — Golem will update the site accordingly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish your site">
    When you're happy with the result, click **Publish**. Your site goes live immediately on a free `yoursite.goleme.ai` subdomain.

    If you want to use your own domain name instead, you can connect one from your project settings. Custom domains are available on paid plans (Business and above).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's next

Your site is live — but Goleme doesn't stop there. You can return to the chat at any time to update text, change colors, add a new page, or request new features. Everything is done through natural language; no code is ever needed.
