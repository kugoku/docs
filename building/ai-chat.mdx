Golem is the AI at the heart of Goleme. You describe the site you want in plain language — the type of site, what pages it should have, what it should do — and Golem builds it for you. There is no drag-and-drop editor or code to write. The chat interface is how you create, refine, and extend your site at every stage.

## Start a new project

Each website you build in Goleme is its own project. To create a new site, you start a new project and open the chat with Golem.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Log in to your Goleme dashboard and click **New project**. Give your project a name — this is just for your reference and won't appear on your site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your site">
    Type a description of the site you want in the chat box. Be as specific as you like: mention the type of site, the pages you need, the content it should include, and any style preferences. The more detail you give, the closer the first result will be to what you have in mind.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Golem to build">
    Hit **Send** and let Golem work. Describing your vision takes around 1 minute; building the site typically takes 1–5 minutes depending on complexity. You'll see a progress indicator while Golem generates your pages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your site">
    Once Golem finishes, a live preview of your site appears. Look through each page and decide what you want to keep, change, or add. You can keep chatting to refine anything straight away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Write a good description

Your initial description is the most important input Golem receives. A clear, specific description produces a site that needs fewer follow-up edits.

**Include in your description:**

- The purpose of the site (portfolio, business, blog, shop, etc.)
- The pages you want (Home, About, Services, Contact, etc.)
- Key content or features (a booking form, a product catalogue, a photo gallery)
- Your preferred tone or style if it matters to you (minimal, bold, professional, friendly)

**Avoid vague prompts** like "make me a nice website." Golem works better when it understands what kind of site it's building and for whom.

<Tip>
  **Example prompts to get you started:**

  - "A simple portfolio site for a freelance photographer with a gallery page, an about page, and a contact form."
  - "A website for a small bakery with a menu page, an about us page, and a contact page with opening hours and address."
  - "A personal travel blog with a homepage showing recent posts, individual article pages, and an about page."
  - "A landing page for a yoga studio with class schedule, pricing, and a contact form for booking a trial class."
  - "A business site for a plumber with a services page, a gallery of completed work, and a request-a-quote form."
</Tip>

## How credits work

Each time you ask Golem to generate or significantly update your site, it consumes AI credits from your account. Credits are separate from your subscription plan — you top them up as needed, and all paid plans allow unlimited top-ups.

You are charged credits once per successful generation. Browsing your site, publishing it, and connecting a domain do not use credits. See the [credits page](/billing/credits) for more detail on pricing and top-ups.
