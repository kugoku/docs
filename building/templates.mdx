If you know the kind of site you need, starting from a template is faster than describing everything from scratch. Goleme's templates give Golem a structured starting point — the right pages, sections, and features are already planned out for that type of site. You still chat with Golem to customize the result, but you arrive at a working site more quickly.

## Select a template

When you create a new project, Goleme gives you the option to start from scratch or pick a template. Select the template that best matches your site type and Golem will use it as the blueprint for your first generation.

You don't need to match your situation exactly — if you run a nail salon and choose **Hair Salon**, Golem will still produce a useful starting point that you can reshape in chat. The templates set the structure; you control the content and details.

## Customize after selecting

Selecting a template does not lock you into a fixed layout. Once Golem has built the initial site from the template, you continue in chat as you would from any other starting point. Ask Golem to rename pages, add sections, change colors, or remove anything that doesn't fit your business.

For example, after generating from the **Hair Salon** template you might type:

> "Add a gallery page with before-and-after photos. Rename 'Booking' to 'Appointments' and change the color scheme to black and gold."

Golem applies the changes and updates your site. You can keep iterating until the result matches what you need.

## Switching templates

Templates are applied at the point of creation. Once Golem has built your site, there is no one-click way to swap the template. If you want to start fresh with a different template, create a new project and select the template you want. Your existing projects are unaffected.
